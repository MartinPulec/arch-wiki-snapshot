Lenovo ThinkPad X13 Gen 5

From ArchWiki

The Lenovo ThinkPad X13 Gen 5 laptop was released in March 2024. It is available with an Intel or AMD Ryzen CPU. Some models include a fingerprint reader, a Smartcard reader, and a WWAN card.


Firmware

Firmware can be updated using fwupd.
