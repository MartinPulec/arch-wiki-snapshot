Usage

After installation the service must be enabled: systemctl enable --now forticlient.service

By default the packages binaries are installed in /opt/forticlient/.

To configure a new profile: fortivpn edit <profile_name>

To connect: fortivpn connect <profilename> -u <username>

To disconnect: fortivpn disconnect <profilename>
