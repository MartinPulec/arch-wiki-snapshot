The ASUS Eee PC 1015PN is a 10.1"-screen netbook computer with dual graphic cards (built-in the CPU and another nVidia card). This article provides some information on hardware specs and some knowledge base about it.

See Laptop and Laptop/ASUS for other laptop-related information.

HDD important issue

See Laptop#Hard drive spin down problem.

Graphics cards

This netbook comes with two graphics cards: one Intel integrated GPU, and a discrete NVIDIA GPU. To see how to manage these graphics cards, see NVIDIA Optimus.

Wireless

For chipset BCM4313, one can choose broadcom-wl or b43, while it is not fully supported by brcmsmac. See Broadcom wireless.
