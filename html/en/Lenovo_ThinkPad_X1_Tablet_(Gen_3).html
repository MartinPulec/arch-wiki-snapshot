The Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Tablet (Gen 3) is the third iteration of Lenovos business 2-in-1 device, close to the X1 series. It 13 inch display has a resolution of 3000x2000 pixels, it comes with two webcams and optionally with a pen. The keyboard has a touchpad and a trackpoint, is fully detachable and has no internal battery.[1]
