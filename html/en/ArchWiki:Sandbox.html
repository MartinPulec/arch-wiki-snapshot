the buggy UEFI firmware of the Aspire 3 a315-56 model

So the firmware of this laptop is like really buggy, and you need to put the laptop to sleep ( suspend to ram ) to even see the internal storage connected to the SATA interface.

the command to do it: 

 echo mem > /sys/power/state

I guess its too much to ask of Acer to see my internal storage without having to constantly put it to sleep and waking it up?
